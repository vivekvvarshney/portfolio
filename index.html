Global Game Jam 2020

Game Premise

You are in coma! You have to relive memories of your life, stitch them together to get a chance revive yourself. Fight time and emotions to bring back your life, piece-by-piece, through your memories, to bring back yourself and live on.

Card game for Mental Health Patients

This is a Social Skills Training card game for Schizophrenic patients inspired by Uno and Dumb Charades. This was based on design research and interviews with psychologists and doctors at various mental health institutions like National Institute of Mental Health And Neuro Sciences, Institute of Psychiatry, Kolkata and Mano Chetana Clinic, Bengaluru.

Genre: Card game,Simulation
Team Size: 4
Duration: 1 month
