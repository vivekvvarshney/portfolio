Global Game Jam 2020

This is my submission to the Global Game Jam 2020 named "7 Memories" with my team for the theme 'Repair'. Inspiration for the game involved exploring mental health, memories and the theme to bring out this as a journey to repair memories as a game mechanic. 

Game Premise

You are in coma! You have to relive memories of your life, stitch them together to get a chance revive yourself. Fight time and emotions to bring back your life, piece-by-piece, through your memories, to bring back yourself and live on.

About Me

Vivek Varshney is an experienced developer, with a knack to explore, think out of the box, and learn from everyday life. Love for games forced him to leave his comfortable 9 to 5 job, in the search to be a part of a project that helps players to live through various imaginations in the medium of games. A post graduation in Game Design, at Srishti Institute of Art, Design and Technology, helped in expanding avenues, add lenses to new perspectives, and deconstruct complex systems into simpler constituents to create streamlined game development and meaningful player experiences.
